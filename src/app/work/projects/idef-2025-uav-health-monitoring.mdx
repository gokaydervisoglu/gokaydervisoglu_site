## Overview

Presented at IDEF 2025, this project introduces an AI-powered real-time fault detection and predictive maintenance system for unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). The system analyzes multi-sensor data during flight to detect anomalies and aims to enhance operational continuity. Designed especially for long-duration and mission-critical operations, the approach improves mission safety while enabling more efficient maintenance planning.

## Core Capabilities

- **Real-Time Fault and Anomaly Detection**: Continuous monitoring of onboard sensor data to identify abnormal conditions during flight and generate critical alerts
- **Predictive Maintenance Approach**: Anticipating maintenance needs based on anomaly analysis to reduce mission cancellation risks
- **Multi-Sensor and AI Integration**: Joint evaluation of data from multiple sensors using artificial intelligence algorithms
- **Compatibility with Multiple UAV Types**: Flexible system architecture capable of operating across different UAV platforms, including fixed-wing systems

## Applied Artificial Intelligence Methods

- **LSTM**: Modeling temporal patterns in sensor time-series data
- **CNN**: Extracting discriminative features from sensor-derived representations
- **Autoencoder**: Unsupervised anomaly detection based on reconstruction error

## Quantitative Indicators (Testing and Performance)

- 5,000+ hours of test data
- 96% anomaly detection success rate
- Up to 40% reduction in maintenance costs
- Flexibility to operate with all UAV types

## Contributions to the Defense Industry

- Enhanced mission continuity
- Reduced operational risks
- Support for proactive maintenance planning
- Strengthening smart and domestic maintenance technologies

## Exhibition

This project was showcased at IDEF 2025 – International Defence Industry Fair to demonstrate the application of artificial intelligence in fault diagnosis and smart maintenance for defense and aerospace systems.
